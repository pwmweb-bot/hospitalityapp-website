Everything in the box

Everything a venue needs in one app.

Rota, compliance, till, kitchen display, menu, stock, gross-profit margins, clock-in, takings and payroll — every module tightly scoped, no upsell tricks. The same data flows from rota → clock-in → labour cost → till → takings → compliance audit, so you never re-key it.

Rota

A rota you can publish before lunch.

Build the week from shift templates with attached opening / mid / closing tasks. Drag-free, role-aware, swap-aware. Staff see only what they're rostered for; managers see the whole week.

  • Shift templates with built-in role tasks (opening, mid, closing phases)
  • Per-staff role chips so the picker only shows eligible team members
  • Pending invites still appear in the assignment picker — start scheduling before they accept
  • Swap requests with manager approval — one tap to approve, the rota reassigns automatically
  • Time-off requests routed to the manager inbox — approve or reject without leaving the app
Compliance · 10 modules

Audit-ready without the binder.

Ten compliance categories, one simple habit: set your recurring checks, log the evidence, snap a photo where it matters — and the app flags anything out of range the moment it's logged. Inspectors get a clean CSV; you keep your weekend.

  • Food safety, fire safety, alcohol licensing, health & safety, workforce, background checks, data protection, environmental, accessibility, insurance
  • Numeric readings with safe min/max bands — pass or fail is flagged the instant it's logged
  • Photo evidence where it counts — checks that need a photo can't be ticked off without one
  • Renewal tracking for insurance, training and licence expiry — alerts before they lapse
  • Daily email digest of overdue and due-soon checks to the owner + active managers
Till / POS

A till built for the venue, not retail.

Drinks-led pages for pubs and bars (half and pint pricing on the same card). Course management and allergen prompts for restaurants. Modifier chains for cafés. The till knows what venue you're in and lays itself out accordingly.

  • Pub / bar / restaurant / café modes — same engine, different default layouts
  • Allergen prompt at order send for any item containing the 14 named allergens (Natasha's-Law ready)
  • Refusal log access from the till — bar staff log under-25 refusals without leaving the sale
  • Split bills (evenly, by item, by seat) — no calculator, no awkwardness
  • Use any card machine — SumUp, Zettle, Stripe, Square or the one you already have. The till records the sale; your terminal takes the payment
  • Bring any iPad or Android tablet — no per-terminal lock-in fees

Stock & margins

Know your stock — and your margins.

Track what's behind the bar and in the cellar, set reorder limits, and let the till do the counting. Every sale depletes stock by the exact measure poured, so your levels stay honest without a clipboard.

  • Live stock levels per item — bottles, kegs, cases — with par and reorder limits
  • Low-stock "to order" list grouped by supplier, so ordering takes minutes
  • Auto-depletion on sale: a linked product drops stock by its pour (ml ÷ container size)
  • Receive, waste, count and adjustment movements — a full, dated stock ledger
  • Stocktakes that reconcile counted vs system in one tap

Gross-profit margins. Set a cost on every product and the till shows its GP% (price − cost) ÷ price. For drinks, enter the container cost and size once — the per-measure cost and margin calculate themselves.

Payroll & takings

Watch labour cost % live.

Log gross takings daily or weekly. The app multiplies rota hours by hourly rate, divides by takings, and shows you the labour-cost % in green / amber / red against your target.

  • Daily or weekly takings cadence — set it once, change it any time
  • Target labour % per business with green / amber / red bands (≤ +2pp ok, +2–5pp warn, > +5pp critical)
  • Forecast band: how much more you need to take to land on target — live, today
  • Itemised payroll export, emailed straight to your bookkeeper and ready to run
  • Per-staff hourly rates, edited on the staff detail screen
Staff & invites

Onboard your team in seconds, not an afternoon.

Send an invite and your new starter onboards themselves — no IT, no paperwork, no shared passwords. They tap a secure link in the email, set their own password, and land straight in your venue, ready for their first shift.

  • Invite by email — a branded link your new starter taps to onboard themselves
  • Pending invitees show a PENDING badge in the rota picker so you can schedule before they accept
  • Per-staff hourly rate, role, manager flag and contract type all editable from one screen
  • Re-send an invite, deactivate or reactivate a team member, hand over ownership — all from one screen
  • Manager inbox bundles swap approvals and time-off requests in one place
Native apps

iOS & Android — built for one-handed use.

The mobile apps aren't a wrapper — they're tuned for staff. Native pickers for time and date, swipe-to-delete tasks, pull-to-refresh, push notifications for swap requests.

  • Tap-don't-type time and date wheels — no fat-fingering 17:00 on a phone keypad mid-shift
  • Pull-to-refresh on rota, inbox and swap requests
  • Swipe-to-delete on task rows, with screen-reader-friendly fallbacks
  • Invite and reset links open straight in the app, not a browser tab
  • Runs on your team's own iPhones and Androids — no company hardware to buy or manage
