What it is

Closing-shift checklist, defined.

A closing-shift checklist is the standardised list of tasks the last team on duty must complete before locking up — last orders called, kitchen secured, fridges checked, takings reconciled, alarm armed, bins out. Done well, it removes the Sunday-morning "did anyone…?" panic and creates an audit trail of who closed what, when.

The trick is keeping it short enough to actually finish at 1am and complete enough to catch the things that hurt at 8am — the unlocked back door, the fridge that didn't get logged, the till discrepancy nobody flagged.

The template

The closedown sequence, in order.

Sequence matters. Last orders before kitchen-off before till before alarm. Build it once, run it every night.

1 · Last orders

Bell rung, time logged. The licensing-officer evidence of trading-hours compliance.

2 · Kitchen off

Hot-hold off, surfaces cleaned, fryer covered. Final cooked-temp check logged.

3 · Cellar locked

Gas off, taps closed, cellar door secured. Brewery rep's contract requirement.

4 · Fridges checked

Closing temperature read across every cabinet, logged with photo of the probe. Overnight risk known.

5 · Till reconciled

Z-read run, cash counted, float separated, takings logged into the app. Bank-run note ready.

6 · Public spaces cleared

Glasses collected, tables wiped, chairs up, floors swept. Photo of the room.

7 · Bins out

General waste, glass, food. Photographed at the kerb. Avoids the 7am "did anyone…?".

8 · Doors & windows

Back door bolted, fire exits locked, windows latched. Photo confirms.

9 · Alarm armed

Set, beep heard, photo of the panel showing armed state. Last task before the front door.

Variations by venue

What each venue adds (or skips).

Pubs (wet-led).

Add: cellar gas off photographed, line-end-of-night purge if applicable, refusal log entries summarised. Skip: kitchen tasks if no food served.

Pubs (food-led) and restaurants.

Add: fryer covered, deep-clean rotation tasks, allergen-matrix re-confirmed for tomorrow's menu, cooked-meat fridge temp logged with photo evidence.

Late-night bars.

Add: door-staff sign-off (SIA badge logged), dispersal-policy evidence (last customer time, complaints log), music-off time. Closing-time evidence is what defends your licence.

Cafés and coffee shops.

Add: espresso machine flush, milk-rotation dated, pastry use-by logged. Skip: most of the licensing items.

Hotels.

Add: night-audit run, fire-panel reading, overnight reservation list confirmed. The hotel closedown overlaps with the night auditor's checklist — usually one combined template.

FAQ

Closing-shift checklists, asked & answered.

What is a closing-shift checklist?

The standardised list of tasks the last team must complete before locking up — last orders, kitchen secured, fridges checked, takings reconciled, alarm armed.

Why digital over paper?

Photo evidence and timestamps stop tasks being ticked through without being done. Creates an audit trail for licensing, food safety and dispute resolution.

What should be on a pub's closing checklist?

Last orders, cellar gas off, beer taps closed, glassware washed, till reconciled, fridges logged, kitchen cleaned (if food-led), bins out, music off, exits secured, alarm armed.
