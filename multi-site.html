FAQ

Multi-site questions.

How does central admin work across venues?

One head-office login sees every venue. Area managers see only the sites they cover. Each venue keeps its own rota, team and till, with reporting rolled up across the group.

Can each site keep its own menu and prices?

Yes. Run a shared group menu, a shared menu with per-site price overrides, or fully independent menus per venue — controlled centrally.

How do permissions work across multiple venues?

Role-based, per venue. Staff, venue managers, area managers and head office each get the access that fits their job, and nothing more.

What does rolling out across a group look like?

Self-serve, one venue at a time. New sites inherit your shift templates and compliance setup, so each launch is faster than the last. No implementation project and no hardware contract.

Is each venue's data kept separate?

Yes. Every venue's data is isolated with row-level security and stored on EU-region servers, exportable or deletable at any time.
