Account

Delete your account.

We process every deletion request manually so we can untangle the parts that have to stay (UK law) from the parts that don't.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at hello@hospitalityapp.co.uk from the address you signed up with.
  2. Include the venue name (so we can locate your account).
  3. We confirm receipt within 2 business days, complete the deletion within 30.

What gets deleted

  • Your name, email and any contact details
  • Your account login and authentication tokens
  • Direct messages you sent or received (1:1 chats)
  • Push notification tokens

What's retained, and why

Some records are retained because UK law or your employer's statutory obligations require it. These are anonymised where possible — your name is removed but the underlying record stays:

  • Shift history & clock-in logs — typically 2 years (Working Time Regulations 1998)
  • Payroll records — 6 years from end of tax year (HMRC)
  • Food-safety / HACCP entries you signed off — kept for the inspection period required by your venue's food authority
  • Alcohol-licensing logs (refusals, training) where you were the named recorder
  • Compliance evidence photos you uploaded — retained per the relevant compliance category's renewal cycle

After the legal retention period expires, we delete or further anonymise the remaining data. If you have specific questions about what's retained for your account, reply to our confirmation email and we'll detail it.

Owners

If you're the venue owner and want to delete the entire business (including all staff, rotas, compliance and event records), email us with the subject line "Business deletion request". We confirm identity, notify your team, and wipe everything within 30 days.
