About

Built for the people behind the bar.

HospitalityApp is one app for rota, compliance, till and payroll — made in the UK, for the independent operators who do six jobs before lunch. Not enterprise chains. Not a stack of disconnected tools. One source of truth.

Why we built it

Run an independent venue and your "system" is usually a wall of tabs: a rota in a spreadsheet, compliance in a paper folder, takings in a notebook, payroll in a third app, and a group chat holding it all together. Nothing talks to anything else, and the person paying for all of it is the one already pulling the longest shift.

We thought the basics should live in one place — and that the app should earn its keep in week one or be deleted. So HospitalityApp puts the rota, the compliance records, the till, daily takings and payroll-ready timesheets behind a single login, on web and on every phone in the building.

What we believe

  • One source of truth. Hours worked feed payroll. Compliance checks attach to shifts. The rota cross-checks itself against leave. Enter it once.
  • Built for owner-operators. The defaults assume a manager who's also on the floor — fast to set up, quick to run, no implementation consultant required.
  • Honest pricing. Per venue, from £49 a month. No per-terminal fees, no per-user fees, no surprise add-ons to unlock the basics.
  • Your data is yours. Operational data is stored in the EU with row-level security per business, and you can export or delete it at any time. The marketing site sets no cookies and our analytics are cookie-free.

Run by real venues

HospitalityApp isn't a mockup. Our first venue, The Foregate, runs its rota, clock-in, compliance records and payroll on it day to day — which is exactly how we want to build: against the messy reality of a working venue, not a slide deck. Every feature has to survive a real Friday night before it ships.

Where it's made

HospitalityApp is a product of Psychemeta Ltd, a company registered in England & Wales, and is built in the UK. The product runs on iOS, Android and the web at my.hospitalityapp.co.uk. We're an early-stage product and still in beta — which means we move quickly, we read every message, and the roadmap is shaped by the people actually using it. If something's missing or broken, tell us and you'll likely see it change.
