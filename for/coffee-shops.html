Why coffee shops choose us

The four problems we solve for specialty bars.

Espresso machine flush log.

Daily group flush, weekly backflush, monthly descale — all on the morning checklist. Manufacturer warranty stays valid; service engineer arrives to evidence, not excuses.

Milk rotation tracked.

Fridge check captures date-stamped milk batches. Oat, soy, almond, whole — first-in-first-out, no panicked Sunday-morning curdle. Allergens logged at the same time.

Barista rota that handles split shifts.

Two on the bar 7–11, one through the lull, two back for the lunch rush. Build a "weekday morning" template, swap-request inbox handles the rest.

Weekly labour-cost % at a glance.

Daily takings in, weekly hours from the rota, labour-cost % per week. Spot the Wednesday wobble before it's a month-end problem.

A coffee shop's compliance categories

Tight focus, no clutter.

Food safety

Fridge / pastry display temps, machine flush, milk rotation, supplier list.

Allergens

Menu matrix, plant-based milk handling, server sign-off. PPDS-ready.

Fire safety

Alarm test, extinguisher inspection, evacuation plan if you have seating.

Health & safety

Hot-surface (steam wand, group head), slip-trip, manual handling for milk crates.

Workforce

Right-to-work, food-handler basics, latte-art training records (optional, useful).

Insurance

Public liability, contents (the grinder isn't cheap), employers' liability.

FAQ

Coffee-shop-specific questions.

What's the difference vs the cafés page?

Coffee shops tend to be coffee-led with a tight food offer (pastries, toasties). The cafés playbook leans broader — cooked breakfast, lunch service. Pick whichever fits your menu; you can switch later.

Does it cover Natasha's Law (PPDS labelling)?

The allergen log holds your matrix and per-product allergen data; the labelling itself happens at the till or label printer. We track PPDS rule changes and will ship a label module when shops need it.

Do you integrate with Square / Lightspeed / iZettle?

Not in v1 — takings entered as gross daily / weekly figures so the app stays till-agnostic. POS integrations are on the roadmap; meanwhile most shops paste a daily total in 30 seconds.
