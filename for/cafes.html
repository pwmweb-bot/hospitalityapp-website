FAQ

Café-specific questions.

I only have three baristas. Is it overkill?

No. Starter is built for tiny teams — the WhatsApp-replacement value lands on day one. Skip Growth until you've got a manager.

Do you integrate with Square / Lightspeed / iZettle?

Not in v1 — takings are entered as gross daily/weekly figures so the app stays till-agnostic. POS integrations are on the roadmap.

What about Natasha's Law (PPDS labelling)?

The allergen log holds your matrix; the labelling itself happens at the till / printer. We're tracking changes in PPDS rules and will surface a label-printing module if cafés ask for it.
