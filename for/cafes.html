A café's compliance categories

Just the bits that matter.

Food safety

Fridge / display temps, cleaning schedule, pastry use-by.

Allergens

Menu matrix, supplier ingredient list, server sign-off.

Fire safety

Alarm test, extinguisher inspection, fryer fire blanket if applicable.

Health & safety

Hot-surface, slip-trip-fall, manual handling for milk crates.

Workforce

Right-to-work, food handler basics, contracts.

Insurance

Public liability, contents, employers' liability if you have staff.

FAQ

Café-specific questions.

I only have three baristas. Is it overkill?

No. Essentials at £49/mo is built for tiny teams — the WhatsApp-replacement value lands on day one. Step up to Professional when you've got a manager and the full compliance file starts to matter.

Do you integrate with Square / Lightspeed / iZettle?

Not in v1 — takings are entered as gross daily/weekly figures so the app stays till-agnostic. POS integrations are on the roadmap.

What about Natasha's Law (PPDS labelling)?

The allergen log holds your matrix; the labelling itself happens at the till / printer. We're tracking changes in PPDS rules and will surface a label-printing module if cafés ask for it.
