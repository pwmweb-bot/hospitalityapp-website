FAQ

Restaurant-specific questions.

Do you replace SFBB / Safer Food Better Business?

The app replaces the diary part — temps, cleaning, fitness-to-work, supplier checks all log here with photo evidence. Keep the safe methods reference; we cover the daily logging.

Can the chef use it without seeing FOH stuff?

Yes. Role chips on staff records mean kitchen team see kitchen tasks; the rota only assigns shifts to staff with the right role.

What about tronc / tip distribution?

Not in v1 — the payroll module exports timesheet hours; tronc is handled by your payroll provider. Tronc reporting is on the roadmap.
