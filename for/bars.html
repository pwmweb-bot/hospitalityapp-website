FAQ

Bar-specific questions.

Does this work for a "wet only" bar (no food)?

Yes. Food-safety category can be reduced to the minimum (ice, garnish prep) — the heavy lifting is in the alcohol licensing, late-night refreshment and door-staff categories.

Can our DPS sign off compliance entries from home?

Yes. Anyone with a manager flag on their staff record can approve, log and edit from web or mobile. Bar staff can log entries but can't change the structure.

What about Challenge 25 evidence?

The alcohol licensing category includes a Challenge 25 policy entry, a refusal log and an age-verification training record per staff member. That's the full evidence pack the licensing officer asks for.
