FAQ

B&B-specific questions.

I'm a sole trader — is this overkill?

No. Even single-operator B&Bs need a food-safety diary and fire-test record. Essentials at £49/mo gives you that in app form, photo-evidenced, exportable. Replaces the paper diary, nothing more.

Do you handle booking-engine integrations?

Not in v1 — we deliberately stay PMS-agnostic. Occupancy is entered manually; the app is about the work you do once a guest has booked, not the booking itself.

Is it ready for a Food Standards Agency audit?

Yes. The food-safety category mirrors the FSA's Safer Food Better Business diary structure, with photo evidence per entry. Tap export, share the PDF, done.
