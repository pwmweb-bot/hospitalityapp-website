FAQ

Pub-specific questions.

Do you handle the brewery's BPI / EPOS data?

Not directly — takings are entered as gross daily / weekly figures so the app stays EPOS-agnostic. Most pubs find that's faster than wiring a feed.

Can my DPS sign off compliance entries?

Yes. Anyone with a manager flag on the staff record can approve, log and edit. Staff (non-managers) can log entries but can't change the structure.

What about underage refusals — do you log those?

Yes. The alcohol licensing category includes a refusal log entry with date, time, reason and optional photo. Inspectors will ask; this is where it lives.
