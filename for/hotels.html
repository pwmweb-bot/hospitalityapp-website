A hotel's compliance categories

What inspectors and OTAs both want.

From the EHO to Booking.com to the fire-risk assessor — one source of truth, photo-evidenced, exportable.

Food safety

HACCP, breakfast service logs, allergen sign-offs, room-service food handling.

Fire safety

Weekly alarm test, monthly emergency lighting, fire-door inspection log, evacuation drill record.

Health & safety

Risk assessments, COSHH (cleaning chemicals), Legionella checks, lift inspection date.

Housekeeping

Room-checked sign-off, linen-temperature log, deep-clean rotation, lost property register.

Workforce

Right-to-work, contracts, training records, BIIAB / personal licence for bar staff.

Insurance & licences

Public liability, employers' liability, premises licence, music licence — with renewal alerts.

FAQ

Hotel-specific questions.

Do you integrate with our PMS (Mews, Cloudbeds, Little Hotelier)?

Not in v1 — we deliberately stay PMS-agnostic so the rota and compliance side works for any independent. Occupancy is entered as a daily figure for labour-cost calc. Direct PMS sync is on the roadmap.

Can different departments see different things?

Yes. Role chips control which categories and rota cells a team member sees — housekeeping doesn't need bar refusals; the night auditor doesn't need allergen matrices.

What about multi-property groups?

The Multi-site plan gives you central admin across two or more hotels with per-property compliance, rota and labour-cost dashboards in one view.
