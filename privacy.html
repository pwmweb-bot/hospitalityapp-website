Privacy Policy.

We collect what we need to run your venue, store it in the EU, and let you take it back any time. This page explains the specifics in plain English.

1. Who we are

Psychemeta Ltd ("we", "us") is the data controller for personal data processed via HospitalityApp — the app at my.hospitalityapp.co.uk and the iOS and Android apps. HospitalityApp is a product of Psychemeta Ltd, a company registered in England & Wales. For privacy matters: privacy@hospitalityapp.co.uk.

2. What we collect

Account data

  • Name, email, password (hashed; we never see the plaintext)
  • The business(es) you own or work at
  • Your role within each business (owner, manager, staff)

Operational data

  • Rota: shifts assigned, hours worked, role
  • Compliance: check definitions, log entries, photo evidence, readings
  • Takings: gross daily / weekly takings figures
  • Payroll: hourly rate, contract type, payslip-relevant calculations
  • Communications: announcements posted, swap requests, read-receipts

Device + diagnostic data

  • Device push token (when you opt in to push notifications)
  • Approximate location at clock-in (only if your business enables it)
  • Crash reports + anonymised performance metrics

3. Why we collect it (legal bases)

  • Contract — to provide the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interest — to keep the service secure, prevent fraud, run anonymised analytics
  • Legal obligation — when required by HMRC, food-safety or licensing inspectors
  • Consent — for push notifications, marketing emails, and optional location capture

4. Where it's stored

All operational data is stored on Supabase (Postgres) in the EU (Frankfurt) region with row-level security per business. Static assets are served from Vercel's global edge. Email is sent via Resend. Push notifications go through APNs (iOS) and FCM (Android). All transit is HTTPS / TLS.

5. Sub-processors

  • Supabase Inc — database, authentication, storage
  • Resend — transactional email (invites, digests, payroll exports)
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) — iOS push delivery
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — Android push delivery
  • Vercel — hosting
  • Plausible — privacy-respecting, cookie-free site analytics

6. How long we keep it

  • Account + operational data — as long as your account is active, plus 30 days after cancellation
  • Compliance logs — kept for 6 years to support HACCP, licensing and tax-record retention requirements
  • Diagnostic data — 90 days
  • Backups — encrypted, rotated weekly, retained 30 days

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can:

  • Ask for a copy of everything we hold about you (data subject access request)
  • Have it corrected if inaccurate
  • Have it deleted (subject to the retention rules above for compliance records)
  • Object to processing for marketing or analytics
  • Withdraw consent for push / location at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk

Email privacy@hospitalityapp.co.uk and we'll respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

The marketing site (hospitalityapp.co.uk) sets no cookies. We use Plausible Analytics, which is cookie-free. The app at my.hospitalityapp.co.uk uses a session cookie strictly necessary for sign-in.

9. Children

The service is not intended for under-16s. If your business invites a young worker (16-18), it's the business's responsibility to confirm they meet UK working-time and right-to-work rules.

10. Changes

If we materially change this policy we'll email account-holders. The "last updated" date at the top of the page always reflects the current version.
