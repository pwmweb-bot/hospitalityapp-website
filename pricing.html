FAQ

Pricing questions.

What does "per venue" mean?

One physical site under one licence. A pub with one address is one venue. A small group with three sites would pick Multi-site.

Do I need a card for the trial?

No. The 14-day free trial works on Starter and Growth without entering payment details. We'll email you before the trial ends.

Can I switch plans?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade any time. We pro-rate on the next bill.

Are staff seats charged?

No. Staff use the app for free. You're charged per active venue, not per user.

VAT?

Prices shown are excluding VAT. UK VAT-registered businesses can reclaim through the usual route.

What about my data if I cancel?

You can export everything (staff, rota, compliance logs, takings) at any time. After cancellation we keep the data for 30 days in case you change your mind, then delete it.
