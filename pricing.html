FAQ

Pricing questions.

What does "per venue" mean?

One physical site under one licence. A pub with one address is one venue. A small group with three sites pays per venue, with volume discounts at five and ten venues.

Do I need a card for the trial?

No — the 14-day trial is free and needs no card to start. Sign up and use everything straight away. If you decide to stay, you add payment details at the end; if you don't, the trial simply ends. Nothing is ever charged automatically.

Which features are available during the trial?

Every trial starts on Complete (the full Venue OS) so you can feel the till, kitchen display and full compliance suite. At the end of the trial we downgrade you to the plan you chose at signup — or to Complete if you don't pick one.

How does the POS add-on work?

£59 per venue per month, available on Professional only (£99 + £59 = £158 total). It isn't sold on Essentials because the till needs the full compliance and labour-cost / payroll integration that only sit in Professional. If you choose Complete (£149), the till and KDS are already included — no add-on needed.

What happens above the included staff allowance?

Each plan includes an active-staff allowance — Essentials 10, Professional 20, Complete 30. "Active staff" means team members with an active account on your venue (deactivated leavers don't count). Per-staff overage above the allowance is published at +£3 (Essentials) and +£3.50 (Professional and Complete), but is not auto-billed today — if your venue grows past the allowance we'll talk to you about it at renewal, not mid-cycle. Staff use the app for free.

Can I pay annually?

Yes. Annual billing gives you two months free — Essentials £490/yr, Professional £990/yr, Complete £1,490/yr. Switch at any renewal.

Do you offer multi-venue or group discounts?

Yes. Five or more venues = 15% off the per-venue rate. Ten or more = 25% off. Multi-venue customers also get cross-venue dashboards and central admin included.

VAT?

Prices shown are excluding VAT. UK VAT-registered businesses can reclaim through the usual route.

What about my data if I cancel?

You can export everything (staff, rota, compliance logs, takings, till sales) at any time. After cancellation we keep the data for 30 days in case you change your mind, then delete it.
