FAQ

What owners ask first.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your data is stored in EU-region Supabase infrastructure with row-level security per business. We're GDPR compliant by default and you can export or delete everything any time.

Do you support multi-site?

Yes — the Multi-site plan includes central admin across two or more venues, with per-venue compliance, rota and labour-cost dashboards.

What does onboarding look like?

Sign up free, add your venue, invite your team. Most owners are scheduling their first week within 10 minutes. We'll import your existing rota if you send it across.

Do you have a free trial?

The Starter and Growth plans include a 14-day free trial — no card needed to start. You can downgrade or cancel any time.

Mobile and web?

Both. Owners and managers tend to live in the web app; staff use the native iOS or Android app for rota, clock-in and compliance entries.
